The Box Model Refernce

Basic information on The Box Model

Name

Tag

Description

Width

width:

The measurement or extent of the content from side to side.

Height

height:

The measurement of the content from the top to the bottom.

Padding

padding:

The space between the contents of a box and the borders of a box.

Border

border:

This is the area between the padding and the margin.

Margin

margin:

This is the space between the border of one element to the border of the other element.
